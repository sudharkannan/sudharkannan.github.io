Summary

I worked in a group of 4 to create a fully automated driving robot from scratch in 6 weeks! The robot used reflectance sensors to follow electrical tape along a race course, where it raced head-to-head against other robots in a Mario Kart themed competition. Our robot did very well during the competition, reaching the semi-finals of the competition. Overall, we very very pleased with our performance, and gained incredibly valuable experience and insight into the design process, as well as technical experience with circuit design and assembly, CAD, mechanical design, control systems, and experience working with microcontrollers.
